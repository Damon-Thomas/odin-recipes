Gingered Beef

Description

Breaded beef strips generously glazed in a tangy ginger sauce. It's sure to be a crowd pleaser!

Make enough for leftovers, and the rice is mandatory!

Ingredients

  • breaded beef strips
  • ginger sauce
  • rice

Steps

  1. start cooking rice
  2. cook beef strips in an airfryer
  3. when beef is done add sauce and mix well
  4. plate rice when done
  5. add sauced beef strips to top of rice
  6. Enjoy!
