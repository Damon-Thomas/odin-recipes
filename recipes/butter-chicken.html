Butter Chicken

Description

A tomato based creamy curry recpie with moist chicken chunks makes for the perfect dish for 1 or large groups.

Spice levels are negotiable. Don't forget to pair with naan bread and rice!

Ingredients

  • chicken breast
  • butter chicken sauce in a jar
  • naan bread
  • rice

Steps

  1. start cooking rice in a ricecooker
  2. chop chicken breast and cook on stovetop
  3. when chicken is almost done, put butter chicken sauce in another pot on stovetop and simmer
  4. cook naan bread
  5. combine butter chicken sauce and chicken breast
  6. plate butter chicken and rice, add naan bread to the side
  7. Enjoy!
