Chicken Strips

Description

A breaded chicken fillet with a light seasoning. The perfect finger food with a fast cook time in the airfryer.

Customize with your favourite sauce, or a sauce bar when you have guests. It just works.

Ingredients

  • chicken fillets
  • bread crumbs
  • egg
  • butter

Steps

  1. just buy them frozen
  2. spread in airfryer basket so they don't overlap
  3. cook following instructions
  4. put desired sauce in sauce bowl
  5. plate chicken strips
  6. Enjoy!
